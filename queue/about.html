About NeuroGuard

Building the future of burnout prevention through high-fidelity physiological monitoring and machine learning.

Our Mission

Empowering mental health through physiological precision.

NeuroGuard was born from the intersection of neuroscience and software engineering. Our mission is to transform subjective mental fatigue into objective data, allowing individuals and organizations to preemptively address burnout before it manifests as physical illness.
